Why OnekoMac

A cat worth keeping

Everything you'd expect from a native macOS app — and a lot you wouldn't.

⚡

Metal GPU · 120 Hz

CVDisplayLink + triple-buffered Metal pipeline. Silky smooth on ProMotion and standard displays. Zero CPU at idle.

🐱

Name your pet

Give your cat a name during onboarding. Generate and share a pixel-art identity card. Your cat, your story.

🎨

Skin Marketplace

5 bundled skins + an in-app marketplace that fetches community skins directly from GitHub — no app update needed.

📐

Resize freely

Scale your cat from 0.5× (tiny) to 3× (giant). Pixel-perfect at every size thanks to nearest-neighbor sampling.

😴

Double-click to nap

Double-click the cat and it runs to the menu bar to sleep. Click it again to wake it up. It remembers nothing.

🌍

5 languages

English, French, Japanese, German and Spanish — with a language picker right in the onboarding flow.
